How To How To Mint Anote

First, why to mint and stake AINT (Anote Infrastructure Token):

  •   By staking 1 AINT, you don't have to worry about your AINT Miner health, it will always be on 100%
  •   By staking 10 AINTs, you will receive 10x basic amount even if you don't have 3 active and confirmed referred users
  •   By staking any amount of AINTs, you receive anotes from a special fund reserved for AINT stakers every 10 minutes according to the number of AINTs you staked
  •   New purposes are being developed all the time

How To Mint AINT?

To mint AINT, you must have a certain amount of anotes. The price of minting aint is expressed in USD ($). This is how to mint AINT:

  •   Open "Mint AINT" tab in your anote wallet
  •   Click "Max" button on the amount of anotes invest or enter amount you want
  •   Click "Calculate" button to see how many AINTs you'll get
  •   Click "Mint AINT" button to mint
  •   Wait for the process to finish
  •   Click "AINT" tab and wait for it to show in your balance

How To Stake AINT?

To stake your AINTs and benefit from it, follow these simple steps:

  •   Click the blue briefcase button in your anote wallet
  •   Under amount enter the amount of AINTs you want to stake
  •   Click "Stake AINT" button to stake

How To Unstake AINT?

To unstake your AINTs, follow these simple steps:

  •   Click the blue briefcase button in your anote wallet
  •   Under amount enter the amount of AINTs you want to unstake
  •   Click "Unstake AINT" button to unstake
